1. What is the total number of new B2B customers by year and segment?
=> SELECT id_year, segment, sum(valeur_d1) FROM bda_activations where segment ='B2B' GROUP BY 1,2;
=====================================
2. What is the total number of active B2C customers for the FTTH offer in December 2025?
=> SELECT SUM(valeur_d1) 
   FROM bda_parc 
   WHERE id_year = 2025 
   AND id_month = 12 
   AND segment = 'B2C' 
   AND split = 'FTTH'
   AND kpi = 'EOP HOME';
=====================================
3. What is the total Month-to-Date (MTD) amount of recharges of type *6?
=> SELECT SUM(valeur_d1) 
   FROM bda_recharge 
   WHERE id_date >= '25/01/2026 00:00' 
   AND id_date < '25/02/2026 00:00'  
   AND split = '*6'
   AND kpi = 'RECHARGE PAR TYPE';
=======================================
4. What is the total number of new customers for the Home offer ?
SELECT id_year, segment, sum(valeur_d1) FROM bda_activations where kpi ='GAS HOME' group by 1,2,3;
=====================================
5. What is the total amount for each recharge type ?
SELECT id_year, split, sum(valeur_d1)  FROM bda_recharge where kpi ='RECHARGE PAR TYPE' group by 1,2;
====================================
6. What is the total amount for each recharge channel ?
SELECT id_year, split, sum(valeur_d1)  FROM bda_recharge where kpi ='RECHARGE PAR CANAL';
=====================================
7. What is the total amount for each recharge tranche?
SELECT id_year, split, sum(valeur_d1)  FROM bda_recharge where kpi ='RECHARGE PAR TRANCHE' group by 1,2;
====================================
8. Give me the total Revenu of each split of B2C and B2B
SELECT id_year, split, segment, sum(valeur_d1) FROM bda_revenu_billing  GROUP BY 1,2,3;
=====================================
9. What the total encaissement for each segment ?
SELECT id_year, segment, sum(valeur_d1) FROM bda_encaissement where kpi ='ENCAISSEMENT' GROUP BY 1,2;
======================================
10. What is the total B2C encaissement broken down by offer?
SELECT id_year, segment, sum(valeur_d1) FROM bda_encaissement where kpi ='ENCAISSEMENT B2C PAR OFFRE' GROUP BY 1,2
